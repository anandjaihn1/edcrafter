Subject Matter Expert Search

Content Research & Curation

Whatever subject you want to offer training in, our team of professional researchers can help with curating content from credible sources including:

• Published papers
• Renowned books
• Interviews with learners and experts
• Websites and open resources

All our content is credible, well-researched, and curated to match your specific needs. AI can do a lot of this, but it cannot be specific to your needs. And we cannot match the speed of AI - so we work together.
